Straight answers.

No fine print. If something here ever stops being true, this page changes first.

How does the service fee work?

Your subscription is the only number we charge you directly. The rate on each tier (8%, 5%, 4%, 3%) is the signup checkout fee, added to the registration sticker at checkout the same way ticketing platforms do it. One number on the receipt, settled through your connected Stripe account. No separate Stripe line, no LEAG line stacked on top of your monthly. Want the org to cover that fee itself so the buyer pays only the sticker? One toggle in billing settings flips it.

Can I switch tiers mid-month?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. No retention call, no "let me check with my manager," no friction.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You keep your current tier's features through the end of the billing period, then drop to the free Pickup tier. Your data stays, your event history stays, your customer list stays.

How do refunds work?

Refunds are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Email support@leag.app with your situation and we'll work through it with you.

What's the difference between Coach and Club?

Same signup checkout fee (5% + $0.50). Coach at $24/mo is built for a single coach running one team: waivers, manual schedule, 1-team roster, one login. Club at $79/mo pays for itself in tools and in multiple coach accounts under one club: custom domain, up to 15 teams, member/parent directory, broadcast email, ACH dues + installment plans, basic schedule generator, bulk roster import, and 5 staff seats. Switch to Club when you bring on assistant coaches, run multiple teams under one brand, or parents start asking for installment plans.

Where does each tier pay for itself?

Coach $24 vs free Pickup: signup checkout fee drops 8% to 5%, so buyers save ~$800/mo in volume before Pickup's $1k/mo cap kicks in (and your sub pays for itself even faster if you choose to absorb the fee). Club $79 vs Coach $24: same 5% rate, so Club pays for itself in tools (custom domain, multi-team roster, broadcast email, ACH, schedule generator, bulk import, 5 staff seats) and in multiple coach accounts under one club. League $199 vs Club $79: 5% drops to 4%, so the $120/mo sub bump is offset by ~$12k/mo ($144k/yr) in registrations either as savings to buyers or recovered margin if you absorb. League also unlocks multi-club rollup, full schedule generator with divisions, white-label, unlimited staff, and 24-hour priority support. Federation: 4% drops to 3% with a custom subscription. Break-even depends on your volume and onboarding scope, so we work the math with you on a sales call.

What's the ACH rate (Club and League)?

Lower than card. ACH is opt-in for big-ticket signups (season fees, multi-month memberships, $500+ camp registrations) where the card service fee starts to feel punitive. Rate card:

  • Club: 2% + $0.50, capped at $50 per registration
  • League: 1.5% + $0.50, capped at $50 per registration

Customer picks card or ACH at checkout. Card stays the default; ACH is the cheaper option for high-ticket items.

What counts toward Pickup's $1,000/mo payment cap?

Total payments processed through your LEAG checkout in a calendar month. Once you cross the cap, you'll be prompted to upgrade to Coach to keep taking signups. Pickup also has a $3 minimum sticker on paid events to keep the free tier honest; free events stay free.
