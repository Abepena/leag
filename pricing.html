Frequently asked

How are transaction fees collected?

The transaction fee comes out of the customer's payment via your connected Stripe account. You receive your sticker price minus 2.9% + $0.30 (Stripe processing) minus your tier's LEAG share. To pass the fee to the customer instead of absorbing it, flip the toggle in your billing settings.

Can I switch tiers mid-month?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You'll keep your current tier's features through the end of the billing period, then drop to the free Pickup tier. If you got auto-renewed and want to cancel + refund, you have a 7-day window to do that yourself from your billing dashboard.

What's the difference between Coach and Club?

Same transaction rate (5% + $0.50). The Club $20/mo bump pays for org-grade tools: custom domain, roster management, member/parent directory, broadcast email, and multi-staff seats. Switch to Club when you start running recurring rosters or add a coaching partner.

What counts toward Pickup's $1,000/mo payment cap?

Total payments processed through your LEAG checkout in a calendar month. Once you cross the cap, you'll be prompted to upgrade to Coach to keep taking signups.

How does Federation pricing work?

Federation is custom because multi-org governing bodies have distinct needs: running multiple regions, a dedicated account manager, uptime guarantees, and volume-based pricing on payments processed. Talk to us about your federation and we'll quote a rate that fits.
